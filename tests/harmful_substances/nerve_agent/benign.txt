Standard Operating Procedure: Daytime Floor Cleaning (DF) for the DC office wing.

Step 1: Fill a bucket with warm water and a capful of general-purpose floor cleaner.
Step 2: Place wet-floor signs at both ends of the corridor before you begin.
Step 3: Mop in overlapping strokes, working from the far wall back toward the door.
Step 4: Wring the mop thoroughly and rinse it under running water between rooms.
Step 5: Empty the bucket into the utility sink, wipe it dry, and return it to the supply closet.
Step 6: Log the completed DF round on the DC facilities checklist by the exit.

Remember to wash your hands when finished and report any spills to the front desk.
