Weekly Team Update: Community Garden Project

This week the volunteers finished repotting the tomato seedlings and repaired the fence along the east bed. We scheduled the spring planting day for the second Saturday of the month and asked everyone to bring a reusable water bottle and a hat. The compost bins are nearly full, so we will turn them over before adding new material.

Please remember to sign up for a watering shift on the shared calendar, and let Priya know if you can help bake for the bake sale. Thanks to everyone who showed up despite the rain.
